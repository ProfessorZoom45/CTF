Cards Received The Flame Carries On

Your 5-card set is in the review queue. The PTG team will evaluate each card for balance, originality, and fit within the CTF canon.

What Happens Next
1
Review Period
The PTG team reviews your submission for balance, name originality, and CTF rules compliance. All 5 cards are evaluated together as a set.
2
Decision & Feedback
You'll receive an email at the address you submitted with an approval, rejection, or revision request. Approved cards may be modified for balance before entering canon.
3
Canon Entry
Approved cards are added to the official CTF card database and credited to your gamertag in the card's metadata. They become part of the permanent CTF set library.
4
Your Email Receipt
An email client window should have opened with your full card details. Send it if it didn't go automatically — it gives PTG a second copy of your submission.
