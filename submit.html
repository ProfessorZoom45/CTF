Submit Custom Cards

Design your own 5-card set — 3 Normal Catalysts, 1 Great Fusion Catalyst, and 1 Trick Card. All cards are reviewed before entering the game.

Custom Card Rules: You receive a stat pool of 4,500 Pressure and 4,500 Counter Pressure to distribute across your 3 non-Fusion Catalysts. Two Catalysts are Level 1–4 (max 1,800 PR / 2,000 CP each). One is Level 6–7 (max 2,500 PR / 2,500 CP). Your Fusion Catalyst is a Great Card combining two of your three — stats cannot exceed materials combined, capped at 3,200 PR, 3,200 CP, or 4,000 total. Your Trick Card targets a single Catalyst — no double effects, max +300 stat boost.
⚠ Important — Please Read Before Submitting Intellectual Property & Ownership: By submitting custom cards, you acknowledge and agree that all approved cards become the intellectual property of Perfect Timing Gaming and Makairis Holding Group (MHG). The final published version of any approved card — including name, stats, effect text, and artwork — is owned exclusively by PTG/MHG and becomes part of the official Carry The Flame canon.

Original Names Required: All card names must be completely original creations. Do not submit names that reference, copy, or are derived from any existing intellectual property — including but not limited to characters, brands, franchises, trademarks, or copyrighted works from anime, video games, movies, TV shows, comics, or any other media. Submissions containing third-party IP names will be rejected.

Subject to Change: All submitted cards are subject to review, modification, rebalancing, renaming, or rejection at the sole discretion of Perfect Timing Gaming. Approved cards may be altered at any time for game balance, legal compliance, or creative direction. Submitting a card does not guarantee it will appear in the game, and PTG reserves the right to modify any aspect of the card before or after publication.

Credit: Approved card creators will be credited by their submitted gamertag in the card's metadata where applicable, but this credit does not convey any ownership rights over the card or its use within the CTF ecosystem.
