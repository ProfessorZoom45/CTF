Victory Conditions

3 Ways to Win. All Active. Every Turn.

I 💥
Chi KO
Reduce Chi from 10,000 to 0

Hit hard, hit fast, drain your opponent's Chi to zero through battle damage and direct attacks. The moment they hit 0 — game over. Instant. No waiting.

II ⚔️
7 Kills
Destroy 7 opponent Catalysts in battle

Grind through your opponent's fighters. Destroy 7 of their Catalysts through pure battle and you win. The battlefield is yours to command.

III 📦
7 Extractions
Capture then extract 7 enemy Catalysts

Capture enemy fighters into your Box by overpowering their defense, then permanently remove them at End Phase. The slowest path — and the most satisfying to land.
