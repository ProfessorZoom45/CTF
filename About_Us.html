How It Started

Started in Buffalo.
Built on nothing
but conviction.

CTF didn't come out of a game jam or an accelerator program. It started because two people from Buffalo, New York believed a real trading card game — original rules, original cards, a real engine — could be built from scratch. No budget. No team. No roadmap handed down from a publisher.

What started as a passion project got treated like a real product. Rules were locked. Systems were tested. Cards were iterated on. The website was built, the deck builder was built, the multiplayer client was built. Over 2,000 cards across dozens of sets, all designed from the ground up and all completely free to play.

That's what Carry The Flame means — not just a game, but a way of working. You carry what matters. You don't set it down because it's hard. You build anyway, in silence, until the work proves itself.

How We Operate

Four rules
we don't break.

01
Free means free.

CTF will never have a paywall, a subscription tier, or a premium card pack. Every card, every match, every patch — free. That is not a tagline. It is a locked design constraint.

02
Feast & Jam — build small, run fast, repeat.

Ship something real. Watch how it behaves. Fix what is broken. Improve what works. Do not wait until it is perfect. Perfect is an excuse to never launch.

03
Quiet moves.

Build in silence. Go public only when you have proof. We do not hype what we have not made. The work earns the attention — the announcement is just confirmation.

04
Consistency over hype.

You can fake excitement. You cannot fake 59 sets of original cards and a working engine. Show up every day. Put the reps in. That is the whole plan.

What CTF Actually Is
The first free custom
online trading card game.

A fully original TCG. Three simultaneous win conditions. A locked 7-phase turn structure. Over 2,300 cards across 59 sets. Playable in your browser. No download. No cost. No catch.

Digital Only

No physical version. No printing. No supply chain. A clean browser experience that works on anything with an internet connection.

Original IP

Every card is original. Original rules, original characters, original art direction. CTF is not a fan game. It is its own thing built from the floor up.

Player-Submitted

Players submit cards. Accepted cards carry their creator's name. If you build with us, your tag goes in the game permanently.
